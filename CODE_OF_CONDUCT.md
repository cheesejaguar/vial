# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Be kind.** Assume good intent. Give constructive feedback. Disagree respectfully.

**Be helpful.** If someone is struggling with a contribution, help them — don't dismiss them.

**Be direct.** Clear, honest communication saves everyone's time. Sugar-coating wastes it.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channel associated with this project.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

---

This is a small project. We don't need 2,000 words of legalese. Just be a decent human.
